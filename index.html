Our Story

Lagos Island's Hidden Gem for Natural Hair

Hollalocs was born from a deep passion for celebrating natural African hair in all its forms. Located on Lewis Street, Lagos Island, we've built a reputation for professional, caring, and affordable beauty services.

Our team of trained stylists specialize in locs, natural hair care, and full beauty treatments — all in a clean, welcoming space that feels like home.

  • Trained & experienced stylists for every hair type
  • Clean, hygienic salon environment always maintained
  • Affordable prices with no hidden charges
  • Walk-ins welcome, appointments preferred
  • Home service available on request
